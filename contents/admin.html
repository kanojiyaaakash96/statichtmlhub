Admin Management System

Task Management

The Task Management module is designed to assist organisations with managing their daily outdoor and office tasks. This module has features such as create, assign and delete tasks if needed. Task report in excel and graphical formats are made available.

Cash Control

This module is designed to help organisations with their petty cash expenses. It has the capability to process cash transactions, expenses, generating vouchers and providing updated reports on company's as well as individual employee's spending habits.

Attendance Management

This is a location based attendance module. No need to wait in a queue. Just one tap on your phone and your attendance is marked. Available on all major mobile platforms, this offers a ubiquitous solution for managing employee attendance.
